Don't miss GradEx 110, OCAD University's 110th Annual Graduate Exhibition ‚Äì Toronto's largest free art and design exhibition from May 7 to 11, 2025. Be inspired by the work of more than 800 emerging artists and designers at OCAD U's 100 McCaul St. location.
